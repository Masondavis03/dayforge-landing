Features

Built different, on purpose.

Every feature exists because scheduling apps have been broken for decades. DayForge is what happens when you start from scratch.

Just say what you need. "Schedule a meeting with the team next Tuesday at 2." DayForge understands natural language, handles conflicts, and slots tasks where they belong. No typing, no tapping, no dragging.
Drop in a syllabus, project brief, or meeting notes. DayForge extracts every deadline, milestone, and deliverable, then builds your timeline automatically. No more manual copying.
AI runs locally on your device using WebLLM. Your schedule, habits, and patterns never leave your machine. No cloud processing, no data harvesting, no compromises on your privacy.
Plans change. DayForge automatically reshuffles your day based on priority, energy levels, and deadlines. Cancel a meeting and watch everything intelligently reflow.
Syncs directly with Canvas to pull assignments, exams, and due dates into your calendar. Built for students who are tired of manually entering every single deadline.
