Features

Built different,
on purpose

Every feature exists because scheduling apps have been broken for decades. We're fixing that.

🎤

Voice-First Scheduling

Just say what you need. "Schedule a meeting with the team next Tuesday at 2." DayForge understands natural language, handles conflicts, and slots tasks where they belong.

📄

Document-Aware

Drop in a syllabus, project brief, or meeting notes. DayForge extracts every deadline and builds your timeline automatically.

🔒

Private by Design

AI runs locally on your device using WebLLM. Your schedule stays yours. No cloud processing, no data harvesting, no compromises.

🚀

Smart Rescheduling

Plans change. DayForge automatically reshuffles your day based on priority, energy levels, and deadlines. No manual dragging required.

🎓

Campus Ready

Syncs with Canvas LMS to pull assignments, exams, and due dates directly into your calendar. Built for students who need it most.
